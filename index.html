Do you offer airport transfers?

+

Absolutely! At Elysium Resort, we provide airport transfers for our guests to ensure a seamless and comfortable journey to our resort. Just share your arrival details, and we'll arrange a convenient transfer, allowing you to begin your Maldivian getaway without delay.

Is there a minimum age requirement for guests?

+

No, there is no minimum age. At Elysium Resort, we warmly welcome guests of all ages. Our resort offers amenities tailored to families with children, including childcare services provided by experienced nannies and a dedicated kids' center. Your little ones can enjoy fun activities and supervised playtime while you relax and unwind during your stay with us.

Do you provide complimentary breakfast?

+

At Elysium Resort, we offer complimentary breakfast for all our guests. However, meals beyond breakfast, such as lunch and dinner, are not included and will be charged separately. Additionally, we provide a complimentary tea snack at 16:45 for our guests to enjoy during their stay.

Do you offer childcare services or kids' clubs?

+

At Elysium Resort, we offer childcare services and kids' clubs to cater to families with children. With dedicated nannies and a kids' center, parents can relax knowing their little ones are in a safe and enjoyable environment. As we don't have a minimum age requirement for guests, families can rest assured that their children will be well taken care of during their stay with us.

Are pets allowed on the premesis?

+

Yes, we do allow pets on the premises at Elysium Resort. However, please note that there will be an additional charge for bringing your pets with you. The charge for having a pet in our villas is $20 per night. We understand that pets are part of the family, and we welcome them to join you on your vacation with us.

What's the check-in and check-out times?

+

At Elysium Resort, our check-in time is 24 hours, ensuring flexibility for our guests' convenience. However, we kindly request that check-out is by 12 pm at the latest. We aim to accommodate your travel schedule while allowing adequate time for our team to prepare for our next guests' arrival.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions or special requests?

+

Yes, we accommodate dietary restrictions and special requests at Elysium Resort. We offer options like vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and dairy-free meals. If guests prefer not to dine in areas with certain aromas, such as beef or pork, our room service can deliver meals directly to their accommodation for a personalized dining experience.
